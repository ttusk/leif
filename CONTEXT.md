# Leif

Leif organizes a concurso study plan and records the learner's progress through it.

## Language

**Concurso**:
The exam or selection process whose study plan Leif organizes.
_Avoid_: Contest

**Matéria**:
A major area of knowledge in a Concurso and one step in its study cycle.
_Avoid_: Subject, discipline

**Assunto**:
An atomic syllabus topic studied within a Matéria.
_Avoid_: Topic, item

**Recurso**:
A study unit or material used to learn a Matéria, such as a PDF, video, or link.
_Avoid_: Study item, item

**Registro de estudo**:
The historical record of one completed or attempted study activity.
_Avoid_: Session, event

**Estado do ciclo**:
The learner's current position in a Concurso study cycle.
_Avoid_: Contest state

**Mural**:
The free-form reference area of a Concurso, including official links and planning notes.
_Avoid_: Wall
